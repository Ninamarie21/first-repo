About Me

Hi! My Name is Niña Marie M. Nacion, I live in Oprra Kalunasan with my parents. I enjoy travelling, dancing and singing. Since I was a kid i dont have any guts nor confidence interacting or socializing other people but i realized doing what you love, everthing feels goood.

Up until fairly recently, I had no idea what it was that I wanted to do. While I have put down communications as my preferred major on the application, that's only because of some recent changes. For most of my life, I went through a lot of different listless moods. I wasn't ever really tied down to one thing. In fact, when I was young I was rather malnourished. I am not at all trying to belittle the challenges that come with weight problems. I've been through a lot of them myself. I grew up associating food with love, and my family was always trying to share a lot of great meals with me. I wasn't exactly the most productive person, and I grew to be what one might call pleasantly plump. I wasn't a stereotypical junk food junkie so much as I was someone who really enjoyed my time with my family at our small home. Therefore, I didn't want to admit that anything was an issue. When I finally got to high school, things had started to change. I made a resolution and actually stuck to it I wanted to have a healthy weight, and I set a goal. I also promised to reward myself by trying something new. Once I had really started to get physically active. I end up joining dance contest. That taught me how much I actually love interacting other people. I've enjoyed my newfound success, and I hope that I can aid other people through my love of dancing as an example not be shy and being intervoert all the time and do what they feel comfortable with.
